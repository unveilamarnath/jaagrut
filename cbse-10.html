What we cover

Subjects and scope

All teaching is concept-first. The diagnostic places each student appropriately. The A-Team builds understanding. The Q-Team tests it independently. You receive a written update every 30 days.

Mathematics
Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Statistics, Probability. Concept-first, with real understanding of proof and process.
Physics
Light, electricity, magnetic effects, carbon compounds. Taught with understanding of the reasoning behind each concept.
Chemistry
Chemical reactions, acids, bases, metals, periodic classification. Conceptual clarity across all topics.
Biology
Life processes, control and coordination, reproduction, heredity. Covered with care and depth.
