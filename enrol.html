    Step two — 1-1 classes

    Individual attention.
    Any board. Any student.

    1-1 classes are available to any student — whether they scored high on the diagnostic or low, whether they are on CBSE or the Australian curriculum. Begin with a free 30-minute trial. No commitment until you have seen the approach.

    1-1 pricing structure

    The minimum purchase is 12 hours. Hours come with clearly communicated use-by dates. Jaagrut’s academic year runs 1 April to 31 March.

    Monthly
    12 hours
    Full price
    Use within 45 days of purchase. Switch to annual after the first month only — not later.
    Quarterly
    36 hours
    5% discount
    Use within 4 months. Minimum 36 hours.
    Annual
    144 hours
    10% discount

    For 1-1: no refunds, except if Jaagrut cancels a class or a teacher becomes unavailable — unused hours are refunded.

    Refund policy — group batches

    Written down.
    Not just promised.

    Within 15 days of classes commencing
    Full refund. No deductions. The 15-day window begins from the date classes commence.
    After 15 days
    Refund proportionate to the syllabus portions not yet tested by the Q-Team. Tested is delivered. Untested is returned. Measured from the Delivery Calendar.
    Diagnostic fee — ₹250
    Non-refundable. Adjusted against your first tuition payment if you join. You do not pay it twice.
    Switching to Annual
    Top up to the annual fee at the 10% discounted rate. Forward-looking, not retroactive.
    Worth saying plainly. Paying for a year does not reduce your refund rights. We earn your trust through what we deliver — regardless of how you paid.
