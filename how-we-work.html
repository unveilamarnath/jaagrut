Our process

Four things we do.
In this order.

Everything at Jaagrut flows from a single discipline — begin with the child, not the syllabus. Understand first. Teach carefully. Test honestly. Stay.

Step 01
We begin by knowing your child

Before a single lesson, your child sits a diagnostic assessment. Not to judge — to understand. Where are the genuine gaps? What comes with ease? What is being held back by a concept not yet fully mastered?

This is not a formality. The diagnostic shapes everything that follows — which batch, which pace, which gaps to address first. The assessment costs ₹250 and is adjusted against your first payment. A detailed written report is delivered regardless of whether you join.

Step 02
We teach — concept first

Our teaching team works through each learning objective with care. No moving on until understanding is confirmed. Every session has a purpose. Every quiz has a consequence.

We consider ourselves students of thinking — and we invite every child to examine the power of thinking and, in time, to surpass us in that skill. Students are placed in Regular or Fast batches based on the diagnostic. 1-1 is available alongside both.

Step 03
A separate team tests — honestly

A different team of teachers — who have not taught your child — sets the assessments, marks the work, and reports the scores. We call them the Q-Team. They have no prior relationship with your child. The score they report is the score your child earned.

Beyond testing, the Q-Team works with students on the things that build a genuinely able mind — learning the lists that matter, writing with clarity, and thinking through problems without being told what to think.

Step 04
We stay — and keep you informed

Every 30 days, you receive a written update — a document on record, not a phone call that disappears. Milestone reviews. Progress against the delivery calendar. When adjustments are needed, we write them down and send them to you.

You will know where your child stands. Not because we tell you what you want to hear — because we write it down and send it to you.
