How we work

Four things we do. In this order.

01
We begin by knowing your child
Before a single lesson, your child sits a diagnostic assessment. Not to judge — to understand. Where are the gaps? What comes with ease? What is being held back by something not yet fully mastered? The honest answer to these questions is where we begin.
02
We teach — concept first
Our teaching team works through each learning objective with care. No moving on until understanding is confirmed. Every session has a purpose. We consider ourselves students of thinking — and we invite every child to examine the power of thinking and, in time, to surpass us in that skill.
03
A separate team tests — honestly
A different team of teachers — who have not taught your child — sets the assessments, marks the work, and reports the scores. We call them the Q-Team. They have no prior relationship with your child. The score they report is the score your child earned.
04
We stay — and keep you informed
Every 30 days, you receive a written update — a document on record, not a phone call that disappears. Progress against the delivery calendar. When adjustments are needed, we write them down and send them to you.
