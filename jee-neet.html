Who this is for

The student who will benefit most

The Class 11 student starting early
The concepts built in Class 11 carry the entire JEE and NEET preparation. Starting with a clear diagnostic picture of where the gaps are makes everything that follows more efficient.
The Class 12 student who needs to consolidate
If your child is in Class 12 and feels uncertain about key topics, the diagnostic maps the gaps precisely — so the remaining time is used where it matters most.
The student who understands but does not score
Speed and accuracy under exam conditions are skills that can be built. We work on these within the Q-Team testing cycle — through timed assessments, viva, and written problem-solving.
The student attempting JEE or NEET a second time
A second attempt benefits most from honesty — finding exactly what did not work the first time and addressing it specifically, not revising everything from scratch.
What we cover

Subjects and scope

All teaching is concept-first. The diagnostic places each student appropriately. The A-Team builds understanding. The Q-Team tests it independently. You receive a written update every 30 days.

Physics

Biology
